Pro Tip for Multiple AI Tools

If you have used more than one AI assistant, here is how to consolidate everything into one clean Glean memory.

⚡ Pro Tip

Combine Responses for Richer Memory

  1. Use the same Glean template in each AI tool you use.
  2. Once you have responses from all of them, open a separate Glean chat and ask it to:
Combine all the responses. Paste each AI tool's response and ask Glean to merge them into one view of you.
Remove any repeated or redundant information. Multiple tools may overlap on the same things. Let Glean clean that up.
Produce one clean, crisp summary. Ask Glean to give you a single, polished output.
  1. Copy that final, consolidated summary into your Glean "memory" chat.
When to Refresh Your Memory

Update your Glean memory any time your context meaningfully shifts:

🔁
Your role or team changes.
📌
Your major projects or priorities shift.
✍️
Your writing preferences change.

You can update as often as you like. Choose whichever option fits your flow:

Option 1 — Chat Update

Start a new chat and say something like:

I would like to update my memory. Here is the latest information about me: [type or paste your updates]

Option 2 — Settings Page

  1. From the Glean home page, click your profile initials in the bottom left corner.
  2. Choose Settings.
  3. Go to Personalization > Memory to review and clean up your saved memories.
  4. A chat opens automatically with the same template options.

You can use the full template, or simply tell Glean what to add, change, or forget in your own words.

Why Set Up Memory Now

Setting up memory gives you four immediate advantages:

🎯

More Relevant Help

Glean can use your real role, work, and preferences across chats and agents that use memory, instead of starting from a generic guess.

ATTN: Fast Mode does not use memory, so it will not be able to pull in your customizations.

✍️

Stronger First Drafts

Drafts come out closer to "ready to send," so you only need to tweak instead of rewrite.

🔇

Less Repetition

You do not have to re-explain your background, projects, or style in each and every conversation.

📡

Better Alignment Over Time

When you update your memory, Glean stays in sync with what you are actually working on presently.
